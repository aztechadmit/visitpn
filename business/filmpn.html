About FilmPN

FilmPN is your mentor and side-kick for filming in Puertas Nuevas. Whether you're a first-timer indie filmmaker, or experienced blockbuster creator, our team will work closely with you and your team to ensure the smoothest operations and least effect to the productivity of your crew. Our services include:
  • Necessary Permit Filing on your behalf (including Film Permits, Nature-Use permits, etc)
  • Location Scouting and Recomendations (before you even arrive!)
  • Equipment Rental and 3rd Party Vendor contracting
  • Extensive "What-to-know" resources and training courses
  • Crew and staffing services
  • Public-space and property reservations
  • Lodging and Accomodations (through affiliate hotels) for all budgets

No Greenscreen Necessary

With thousands of acres of preserved gorgeous natural beauty within mere minutes of the city, wow audiences of your next film with cavernous caves, mile-high mountains, and lush forests. Puertas Nuevas is located on an ecological melting pot with dozens of unique biomes to explore.

Before you film, a Nature-Use permit must be obtained from the Department of Environment. No worries, FilmPN will help you at every step of the way!



Equipment Needed? We got it!

With us, there's no need to check extra luggage. FilmPN works with many independant vendors to provide you with state-of-the-art technology and equipment. Before you arrive, provide us with a list of the equipment you want and we will do our best to source it from our vendors so it's ready to go once you arrive. Our vendors include Globe-City Studio Services, QuickShoot, and others.
