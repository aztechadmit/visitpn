Puertas Nuevas Visitor Information Website

As per Minecraftian Constitution Amendment B12207-9-6A, it is the requirment of all commercial operations to create and post a MCBPO (Minecraftian Business Practice Operation) form designating the purpose, scope, methods, and approach of certain business operations in a manner legible to common readers.

This website is a business operation in regards to pulic outreach and advertisment. See form below.

Purpose

The porpose of this website is to provide useful information in a digestible manner to visitors and guests coming into Puertas Nuevas. Our definition of "visitor" or "guest" applies to those visiting for both commercial / business and personal purposes.

In addition to presenting useful information to visitors, this website is also designed to generate profit in the form of advertising of local PN businesses - see methods section for more information.

Scope

The scope of this MCBPO covers this website (Puertas Nuevas Visitor Information Portal) only and does not apply to any other operations within the Ministry of Recreation nor the Department of Tourism & Hopsitality.

Scope of Operation

This website is designed for visitors and guests not local to Puertas Nuevas. This includes those who have and have not been to the province before. Visitors and guests includes those visiting for commercial, business, and personal purposes.

Methods

In order to present information to visitors, this website is structured such that guests can easily find what they may be looking for, whether that be locations for corperate events, hotspots to visit on a vacation, or hotels and accomodations. This website also prevents an extensive list of attractions, activities, hotels, restaurants, and more which may be attractive to visitors.

Profit Making Approach

As part of keeping this website active, the DTH has added advertising features to generate profit. There are multiple types of advertisments on this site:
  • Advertising of Attractions, Restaurants, Activites, Etc. on 'Things-to-do' page
  • Advertising of services such as rental cars, filming services, event services, etc.

Advertising Priority

In terms of the 'Things-to-do' sections, 3rd party advertisers do not get priority over other attractions when being displayed to website visitors. However, paid advertisments may appear more often in other sections of the site if their tags closely match that section.

On sections of the site detailing goods and services, the 3rd party services displayed are exclusively paid advertisements. However, these businesses have met DTH quality and customer service standards ensuring the best experience for our guests.

Responsible Owners
